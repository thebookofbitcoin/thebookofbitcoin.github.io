Bitcoin Services

ChangeTip

The ChangeTip service provides a way to send Bitcoin denominated tips to other people via their social media personas. ChangeTip was started in December of 2013, and it soon took over as the dominant Bitcoin tipping mechanism on Reddit. The ChangeTip service includes a wide variety of supported tipping platforms: GitHub, Google+, Reddit, StockTwits, Tumblr, Twitter, and YouTube are supported.
